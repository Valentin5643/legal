2. Billing and Payment

2.1 Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Google Play Store. Liftrix does not directly handle or store payment information. By subscribing, you agree to:

  • Google Play's Terms of Service
  • Google's payment processing terms
  • Applicable taxes and fees in your region

2.2 Automatic Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled before the renewal date:

  • Renewal occurs 24 hours before the current period ends
  • Payment method on file is charged automatically
  • Renewal price may differ if subscription price has changed
  • You will be notified of price changes at least 24 hours before renewal
  • Continued use after price change notice constitutes acceptance

2.3 Payment Failures

If automatic renewal payment fails:

  • You will be notified via email and in-app notification
  • Grace period of 7 days to update payment method
  • Premium features remain accessible during grace period
  • After grace period, account reverts to free plan
  • Subscription can be reactivated by updating payment method

2.4 Taxes

Subscription prices may be subject to:

  • Value Added Tax (VAT) in EU countries
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST) in applicable regions

  • Other local taxes as required by law

Tax amounts are calculated and collected by Google Play at checkout. Final price including taxes is shown before purchase confirmation.

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time through:

Method 1: Google Play Store (Recommended)

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap Menu → Subscriptions
  3. Select Liftrix subscription
  4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
  5. Confirm cancellation

Method 2: In-App

  1. Open Liftrix app
  2. Navigate to Settings → Subscription
  3. Tap "Manage Subscription" (redirects to Google Play)
  4. Follow steps to cancel

3.2 Effect of Cancellation

When you cancel your subscription:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • You retain premium access until the period ends
  • No refund for the unused portion of the current period
  • Account automatically converts to free plan after period ends
  • All created content and data are retained
  • No loss of workout history or progress tracking

3.3 Reactivation

After cancellation, you may reactivate your subscription:

  • Before period ends: Reactivation resumes at current billing cycle
  • After period ends: New subscription starts with new billing cycle
  • Previous premium features are restored immediately upon payment
  • Free trial is not available for reactivations

4. Refund Policy

4.1 Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests are considered on a case-by-case basis. You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • You were charged incorrectly or unexpectedly
  • Technical issues prevented you from accessing premium features
  • Subscription was purchased by mistake
  • You experienced app defects that made premium features unusable
  • Other exceptional circumstances at our discretion

4.2 Refund Request Timeline

Refund requests must be submitted:

  • Within 48 hours of purchase for accidental subscriptions
  • Within 7 days for technical issues or defects
  • As soon as possible after discovering incorrect charges

Requests submitted after these periods may be denied.

4.3 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Change of mind after free trial period ended
  • Failure to cancel before automatic renewal
  • Unused portion of subscription after use of premium features
  • Account termination due to Terms of Service violations
  • Purchases made more than 48 hours ago (unless technical issue)
  • Partial refunds for time not used in a billing period

4.4 How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Request Through Google Play (Try First)

  1. Visit Google Play Support
  2. Follow instructions to request refund for in-app purchase
  3. Google may approve refund directly within 48 hours

Step 2: Contact Liftrix Support (If Google Denies)

Email [Support Email] with:

  • Subject line: "Refund Request - [Order Number]"
  • Your Google Play order number (found in purchase email)
  • Registered email address
  • Reason for refund request with details
  • Date of purchase
  • Screenshots of any technical issues (if applicable)

4.5 Refund Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed through Google Play Store
  • Amount refunded to original payment method
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days for credit to appear
  • Premium access is revoked immediately upon refund
  • You will receive email confirmation of refund

4.6 Abuse of Refund Policy

We reserve the right to deny refund requests and suspend accounts that:

  • Repeatedly request refunds after using premium features
  • Demonstrate pattern of subscribing and refunding
  • Submit false or misleading refund claims
  • Abuse the refund system to obtain free premium access

You may upgrade from a lower tier to a higher tier at any time:
