Community Guidelines

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Our Mission: Liftrix is a community-driven fitness platform designed to help individuals achieve their fitness goals while connecting with like-minded people. These Community Guidelines establish the standards of behavior we expect from all users to maintain a safe, respectful, and supportive environment.

1. Core Principles

Our community is built on the following principles:

  • Respect: Treat all community members with dignity and respect, regardless of their fitness level, background, or personal characteristics
  • Safety: Prioritize the physical and emotional safety of yourself and others
  • Authenticity: Share genuine experiences and avoid misleading or deceptive content
  • Support: Encourage and motivate others in their fitness journeys
  • Responsibility: Take ownership of your actions and their impact on the community

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

2.1 Harassment and Bullying

We have zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or intimidation in any form. Prohibited behaviors include:

  • Targeted attacks on individuals based on personal characteristics
  • Body shaming or negative comments about physical appearance
  • Unwanted sexual advances or inappropriate messages
  • Threats of violence or physical harm
  • Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
  • Stalking or persistent unwanted contact
  • Creating accounts to harass users who have blocked you

Examples of Prohibited Behavior:

  • Commenting "you look fat" or "you'll never make progress" on someone's progress photo
  • Sending repeated unsolicited messages after being asked to stop
  • Sharing screenshots of private conversations to mock or shame someone
  • Creating fake accounts to bypass blocks

2.2 Hate Speech and Discrimination

Content that promotes hate, discrimination, or violence against individuals or groups based on protected characteristics is strictly prohibited. This includes content targeting:

  • Race, ethnicity, or national origin
  • Religion or religious beliefs
  • Gender identity or expression
  • Sexual orientation
  • Disability or medical condition
  • Age
  • Body type or size

2.3 Sexually Explicit Content

Liftrix is a fitness-focused platform. The following types of sexual content are prohibited:

  • Nudity (partial or full, excluding appropriate fitness/progress photos)
  • Sexually explicit or suggestive images
  • Sexual acts or simulations
  • Content focused on sexual gratification
  • Sexualized comments or descriptions

Progress photos showing appropriate athletic wear (sports bras, shorts, swimwear) are permitted when the focus is clearly fitness-related and not sexualized.

2.4 Violence and Dangerous Content

Do not post content that depicts, promotes, or glorifies:

  • Violence or physical harm to people or animals
  • Self-harm or suicide
  • Dangerous exercise techniques that pose significant injury risk
  • Use of performance-enhancing drugs or prohibited substances
  • Eating disorders or unhealthy weight loss methods
  • Extreme diets or fasting that may cause harm

Examples of Dangerous Content:

  • Tutorials on using anabolic steroids or growth hormones
  • Promoting extreme calorie restriction or "crash diets"
  • Encouraging exercise through injury or severe pain
  • Sharing tips for hiding eating disorder behaviors

2.5 Misinformation and False Health Claims

Do not post content that contains:

  • Demonstrably false health or fitness information
  • Unsubstantiated medical claims
  • Promotion of unproven supplements or treatments
  • Conspiracy theories about health or fitness
  • Claims that specific exercises can cure diseases
  • Misrepresentation of qualifications or credentials

While users may share personal experiences and opinions, content presenting itself as factual advice should be accurate and supported by credible sources.

2.6 Spam and Commercial Activity

Liftrix is a community platform, not a marketplace. Prohibited commercial activities include:

  • Posting repetitive or unsolicited promotional content
  • Sending bulk messages advertising products or services
  • Using the platform primarily to drive traffic to external sites
  • Multi-level marketing or pyramid scheme promotion
  • Soliciting financial contributions or donations
  • Unauthorized affiliate marketing

Note: Certified fitness professionals may share credentials and general availability, but aggressive promotion or direct solicitation is prohibited.

2.7 Impersonation and Deceptive Practices

You may not:

  • Impersonate another person, organization, or entity
  • Misrepresent your identity, credentials, or affiliations
  • Use misleading profile information
  • Post fake progress photos or stolen content
  • Create fake accounts or personas
  • Manipulate timestamps or data to misrepresent progress

2.8 Intellectual Property Violations

Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Do not post:

  • Copyrighted content without permission (images, videos, music, text)
  • Workout programs or training plans that belong to others
  • Content that infringes trademarks or other proprietary rights

Only share content you have created or have explicit permission to use.

2.9 Illegal Activity

Do not use Liftrix to facilitate, promote, or engage in illegal activities, including but not limited to:

  • Sale or distribution of controlled substances
  • Promotion of illegal activities
  • Circumventing laws or regulations
  • Fraud or financial crimes

3. Acceptable Use Standards

3.1 Progress Photos and Body Images

Acceptable:

  • Progress photos in appropriate athletic wear (sports bras, shorts, swimwear)
  • Before/after comparisons showing fitness transformation
  • Photos focused on muscle development or physique changes
  • Images that clearly demonstrate fitness-related progress

Not Acceptable:

  • Underwear or lingerie photos
  • Photos with suggestive poses or camera angles
  • Images focused on sexualized body parts
  • Photos that are clearly not fitness-related

3.2 Workout Sharing and Advice

Encouraged:

  • Sharing your workout routines and experiences
  • Asking for form checks and technique advice
  • Discussing training philosophies and approaches
  • Celebrating personal records and achievements
  • Providing constructive feedback when requested

Discouraged:

  • Giving unsolicited medical or injury advice
  • Diagnosing injuries or conditions
  • Recommending specific treatments without qualifications
  • Claiming your approach is the only correct method
  • Shaming others for their training choices

3.3 Social Interactions

When interacting with other community members:

  • Be supportive: Encourage others and celebrate their progress
  • Be constructive: If offering criticism, make it helpful and kind
  • Be respectful: Remember that everyone is at a different stage of their journey
  • Be genuine: Share authentic experiences and avoid exaggeration
  • Be mindful: Consider how your words might affect others

3.4 Privacy and Consent

Respect the privacy of others:

  • Do not share photos or information about others without their consent
  • Do not screenshot and share private conversations
  • Respect privacy settings and blocked relationships
  • Do not reveal personal information about other users
  • Ask permission before tagging others in posts

4. Reporting Violations

4.1 How to Report

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these Guidelines, you can report it through:

  • The in-app reporting feature on posts, comments, or profiles
  • Email to [Support Email]

When reporting, please provide:

  • A clear description of the violation
  • Screenshots or specific examples (if applicable)
  • The username or content link
  • Any additional context that may be helpful

4.2 Report Review Process

All reports are reviewed by our moderation team. We aim to:

  • Review reports within 24 hours of submission
  • Take appropriate action based on the severity of the violation
  • Notify reporters when action has been taken (while respecting privacy)

Due to privacy considerations, we may not be able to disclose specific actions taken against other users.

4.3 False Reporting

Submitting false reports with the intent to harass others or abuse the reporting system is a violation of these Guidelines. Users who repeatedly submit false reports may face account restrictions.

5. Enforcement and Consequences

5.1 Content Removal

Content that violates these Guidelines may be:

  • Removed immediately without notice
  • Hidden pending review
  • Restricted from public visibility

Content that receives multiple reports from different users may be automatically hidden until reviewed by moderators.

5.2 Account Actions

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may take the following actions:

Warning

  • Issued for first-time or minor violations
  • Provides education about Community Guidelines
  • No impact on account functionality

Temporary Restriction

  • Limited ability to post, comment, or interact
  • Duration typically ranges from 24 hours to 30 days
  • Applied for repeated minor violations or single moderate violations

Account Suspension

  • Temporary loss of access to the platform
  • Applied for serious or repeated violations
  • May range from days to months
  • Account remains intact but inaccessible during suspension

Permanent Ban

  • Permanent removal from the platform
  • Applied for severe violations, illegal activity, or repeated serious offenses
  • Account data may be deleted
  • Creation of new accounts may be prohibited

5.3 Factors Considered in Enforcement

When determining appropriate action, we consider:

  • Severity of the violation
  • Intent (accidental vs. deliberate)
  • Previous violation history
  • Harm caused to other users
  • Pattern of behavior
  • User's cooperation with moderation team

5.4 Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was taken in error, you may appeal by:

  • Contacting [Support Email] within 14 days of the action
  • Providing your username and a clear explanation of why you believe the action was incorrect
  • Including any relevant evidence or context

Appeals are reviewed by a separate moderation team member and decisions are typically provided within 5 business days.

6. Special Considerations

6.1 Mental Health and Wellness

We recognize that fitness and mental health are interconnected. If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health crisis:

  • Contact local emergency services or a mental health crisis hotline
  • Report content that promotes self-harm or suicide
  • Use our platform's wellness resources (if available)

Remember that Liftrix is not a substitute for professional mental health support.

6.2 Certified Professionals

If you are a certified fitness professional, personal trainer, nutritionist, or healthcare provider:

  • You may indicate your credentials in your profile
  • You must ensure any claims or credentials are accurate and verifiable
  • You remain subject to all Community Guidelines
  • Professional advice should be general in nature and not constitute a professional relationship
  • You may not use the platform primarily for commercial solicitation

6.3 Minors on the Platform

Users aged 13-17 are permitted to use Liftrix with parental consent. To protect minors:

  • Adults must not engage in inappropriate communication with minors
  • Content targeting minors must be age-appropriate
  • Report any suspected exploitation or inappropriate behavior toward minors immediately

7. Platform-Specific Guidelines

7.1 AI Coach Feature

When using the AI coaching feature:

  • Do not attempt to manipulate or "jailbreak" the AI
  • Do not use the AI to generate prohibited content
  • Understand that AI responses are not professional advice
  • Report AI responses that provide dangerous or inappropriate guidance

7.2 QR Code Gym Buddy Feature

When connecting with gym buddies via QR code:

  • Only scan QR codes from people you know and trust
  • Do not share your QR code publicly or with strangers
  • You may disconnect from gym buddies at any time
  • Gym buddy connections should be genuine and not used for spam or harassment

7.3 Comments and Engagement

When commenting on posts:

  • Keep comments relevant to the post
  • Be respectful even when disagreeing
  • Do not spam identical comments across multiple posts
  • Do not use comments to advertise or promote external content

8. Updates to Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time to reflect changes in:

  • Platform features and functionality
  • Legal requirements
  • Community standards and expectations
  • Emerging issues or trends

Material changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email to registered users
  • Prominent notice on the platform

Continued use of Liftrix after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Guidelines.

9. Contact and Questions

If you have questions about these Community Guidelines or need clarification on specific situations, please contact us at:

Email: [Support Email]

We encourage open communication and are here to help create a positive community experience for all users.

10. Acknowledgment

By using Liftrix, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by these Community Guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account restrictions, or permanent removal from the platform.

Thank you for being part of the Liftrix community and helping us maintain a safe, supportive, and inspiring fitness environment.
