3. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link via email. If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

Q: Why isn't my workout data syncing?

A: Ensure you have an active internet connection and are logged in. Data syncs automatically when online. If sync issues persist, try logging out and back in. If the problem continues, contact support with details about when the sync last worked.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: Subscriptions are managed through Google Play Store. To cancel: Open Google Play Store â†’ Menu â†’ Subscriptions â†’ Liftrix â†’ Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. For more details, see our Refund & Subscription Policy.

Q: Can I get a refund for my subscription?

A: Refunds are subject to Google Play Store's refund policy and our Refund Policy. Requests must be made within 48 hours of purchase. Contact [Support Email] with your order number and reason for the refund request.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Navigate to Settings â†’ Account â†’ Delete Account. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. We recommend exporting your data first. See our Account & Data Deletion Policy for full details.

Q: How do I report inappropriate content?

A: Tap the three-dot menu on any post, comment, or profile and select "Report." Choose the appropriate reason and provide details. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24-48 hours.

Q: Why was my content removed?

A: Content is removed when it violates our Community Guidelines or Terms of Service. You should receive a notification explaining the reason. If you believe the removal was in error, you can appeal by emailing [Support Email] with "Appeal - Content Removal" in the subject line.

Q: How do I export my workout data?

A: Go to Settings â†’ Account â†’ Export My Data. Select the data types you want to export and choose your preferred format (JSON or CSV). You'll receive a download link via email within 48 hours.

Q: Is the AI Coach feature safe to use?

A: The AI Coach provides general fitness information only and is NOT a substitute for professional medical or fitness advice. Always consult qualified professionals before beginning any exercise program. See our AI Disclaimer for full details and limitations.

Q: How do I connect with a gym buddy via QR code?

A: Navigate to Social â†’ Gym Buddies â†’ Add Buddy â†’ Scan QR Code. Point your camera at your friend's QR code to create a mutual connection. QR codes expire after 5 minutes for security. You can have up to 5 gym buddies at a time.

Q: What are the app's system requirements?

A: Liftrix requires Android 8.0 (API level 26) or higher. The app works on phones and tablets. Internet connection is required for sync and social features, but you can log workouts offline.
