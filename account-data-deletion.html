Account & Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

IMPORTANT NOTICE

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all data, content, and social connections associated with your account. This action cannot be undone.

Please read this policy carefully before requesting account deletion. We recommend exporting your data before deletion if you wish to retain a copy.

1. Your Rights Under Data Protection Laws

1.1 Right to Erasure

Under applicable data protection laws including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Have your account permanently removed from our systems
  • Receive confirmation when deletion is complete
  • Obtain a copy of your data before deletion

1.2 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to:

  • Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Export your workout data, progress photos, and other content
  • Transfer your data to another service (where technically feasible)

1.3 Scope of Rights

These rights apply to personal data collected and processed by Liftrix. Certain exceptions may apply as required or permitted by law, including data that must be retained for legal, regulatory, or security purposes.

2. Account Deletion Process

2.1 How to Delete Your Account

You can initiate account deletion through two methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Liftrix app and navigate to Settings
  2. Select "Account" or "Account Settings"
  3. Scroll to "Delete Account" option
  4. Tap "Delete Account" and follow the prompts
  5. Verify your identity by entering your password
  6. Confirm deletion by reviewing the consequences
  7. Optional: Export your data before confirming
  8. Tap final "Delete Account" button to submit request

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to [Support Email] with:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your username or account ID
  • Confirmation that you understand deletion is permanent

For security purposes, we will send a verification link to your registered email address to confirm the deletion request.

2.2 Identity Verification

To protect your account from unauthorized deletion, we require:

  • Password verification (for in-app deletion)
  • Email confirmation link (for email requests)
  • Response to security questions (if account access is compromised)
  • Additional verification if suspicious activity is detected

2.3 Deletion Timeline

Immediate (Upon Confirmation):

  • Account access is revoked
  • Profile is hidden from public view
  • User is logged out of all devices

Within 24 Hours:

  • Authentication credentials deleted (Firebase Auth account removed)
  • Account marked for permanent deletion
  • Deletion process begins for user data

Within 7 Days:

  • Personal data deleted from active systems
  • Profile photos and progress photos deleted from storage
  • Workout data and exercise logs deleted
  • Social content anonymized or removed

Within 30 Days:

  • Data removed from backup systems
  • Deletion process completed
  • Confirmation email sent to user

2.4 Cancellation Period

Once you confirm account deletion, you have a 24-hour grace period to cancel the request by:

  • Logging back into your account (if credentials still valid)
  • Contacting support at [Support Email] immediately

After 24 hours, deletion cannot be reversed.

3. What Gets Deleted

3.1 Personal Information

The following personal data is permanently deleted:

  • Email address and authentication credentials
  • Display name and profile information
  • Profile photo and banner image
  • Biography and personal description
  • Age, height, weight, and body measurements
  • Fitness goals and preferences
  • Contact information and social links

3.2 Fitness and Health Data

All fitness-related data is deleted, including:

  • Workout logs and exercise history
  • Sets, reps, and weight progression data
  • Personal records and achievements
  • Progress photos and body composition tracking
  • Workout templates and custom routines
  • Exercise preferences and equipment access
  • Fitness analytics and calculated metrics

3.3 Social Content and Interactions

Social content is handled as follows:

  • Workout posts: Anonymized (author shown as "Deleted User") or deleted
  • Comments: Anonymized or deleted
  • Likes and engagement: Removed from all posts
  • Follow relationships: All connections severed
  • Gym buddy connections: Disconnected from all buddies
  • Private messages: Deleted from your side; recipients retain their copy
  • Shared content: Anonymized or removed

3.4 App Usage and Preferences

System data deleted includes:

  • Device tokens and push notification preferences
  • AI chat conversation history
  • Widget configurations and dashboard layouts
  • Privacy and notification settings
  • Language and unit preferences
  • Sync queue and offline data

3.5 Media Files

All uploaded media is deleted, including:

  • Profile photos
  • Progress photos (all dates and angles)
  • Shared images in posts
  • Thumbnails and cached images
  • Media stored in cloud storage (Firebase Storage)

4. What Does NOT Get Deleted

4.1 Legally Required Retention

Certain data may be retained to comply with legal obligations:

  • Transaction records (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Payment history and subscription records (minimum 7 years)
  • Records necessary for ongoing legal proceedings
  • Data subject to regulatory retention requirements

4.2 Security and Fraud Prevention

Limited data may be retained for security purposes:

  • Account deletion timestamp and IP address
  • Records of Terms of Service violations
  • Security incident logs
  • Fraud prevention identifiers (to prevent ban evasion)

This data is anonymized where possible and retained only as long as necessary for the stated purpose.

4.3 Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We may retain aggregated, anonymized data that cannot be used to identify you, such as:

  • General usage statistics and trends
  • Aggregated fitness metrics (e.g., "average workout duration across users")
  • Platform performance data

This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

4.4 Third-Party Data

Data held by third parties may be subject to their retention policies:

  • Google Play Store transaction records
  • Firebase Analytics data (subject to Firebase retention settings)
  • Crashlytics error logs (automatically expire after 90 days)

We will make commercially reasonable efforts to request deletion from third parties where applicable.

4.5 Other Users' Content

Content belonging to other users is not deleted:

  • Messages received by other users (recipients retain their copies)
  • Comments or posts about you by other users
  • Screenshots or saved content from your public posts

Once content is shared publicly or with others, we cannot control copies made by other users.

5. Data Export Before Deletion

5.1 Export Process

We strongly recommend exporting your data before deletion. You can request a data export through:

  • In-app: Settings → Account → Export My Data
  • Email: Send request to [Support Email] with subject "Data Export Request"

5.2 Export Contents

Your data export will include:

Data Category Format Contents
Profile Information JSON Account details, preferences, settings
Workout Data JSON, CSV Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, dates
Progress Data JSON, CSV Body measurements, analytics, achievements
Social Content JSON Posts, comments, followers list
Media Files ZIP Profile photos, progress photos (original quality)
AI Chat History JSON, TXT Conversation logs with AI coach

5.3 Export Delivery

Data exports are delivered via:

  • Secure download link sent to your registered email
  • Link valid for 7 days
  • Export generation typically completes within 48 hours
  • Large exports may take longer (notification provided)

5.4 Export Limitations

Data exports do not include:

  • Content created by other users (even if about you)
  • Internal system logs or metadata
  • Deleted content (already removed from systems)
  • Aggregated analytics (only your individual data)

6. Consequences of Account Deletion

6.1 Loss of Access

You will permanently lose:

  • Access to your account and all associated data
  • All workout history and progress tracking
  • Social connections with other users
  • Access to premium features (if subscribed)
  • Achievements, milestones, and personal records
  • Custom workout templates and routines

6.2 Impact on Subscriptions

If you have an active subscription:

  • Subscription access ends immediately upon account deletion
  • No refunds are provided for remaining subscription period
  • Subscription auto-renewal is canceled
  • You must cancel subscription separately through Google Play Store to prevent future charges

We recommend canceling your subscription before deleting your account to ensure no further charges occur.

6.3 Impact on Other Users

Your deletion affects others in the following ways:

  • Former gym buddies lose connection to your account
  • Your public posts show "Deleted User" as author
  • Comments appear as from "Deleted User"
  • Followers/following counts update for connected users
  • Shared workout templates become unavailable

6.4 Creating a New Account

After deletion:

  • You may create a new account using the same email address after 30 days
  • New account will have no connection to previous account
  • Previous data cannot be restored or transferred
  • Username may become available for others to use

7. Alternative Options to Full Deletion

7.1 Account Deactivation (Temporary)

If you're unsure about permanent deletion, consider temporarily deactivating your account:

  • Account is hidden from other users
  • You cannot access the app during deactivation
  • Data is preserved and can be restored by reactivating
  • Available in Settings → Account → Deactivate Account

Note: Deactivation is not currently available; this may be added in future updates.

7.2 Privacy Settings Adjustment

If privacy is your concern, you can:

  • Set profile to Private (only you can see your content)
  • Remove all followers and following
  • Delete individual posts and photos
  • Disable social features entirely
  • Block specific users

7.3 Selective Data Deletion

You can delete specific data without deleting your account:

  • Delete individual workouts or posts
  • Remove progress photos
  • Clear AI chat history
  • Disconnect from specific gym buddies
  • Revoke specific data permissions

8. Regaining Access After Deletion

8.1 Recovery Window

Account deletion can be canceled within 24 hours of confirmation by:

  • Logging back in with your credentials
  • Clicking the "Cancel Deletion" link in the confirmation email
  • Contacting support immediately

8.2 No Recovery After Deletion

Once the deletion process is complete:

  • Account cannot be recovered or restored
  • Data cannot be retrieved from backups
  • Support cannot reverse the deletion
  • You must create a new account to use the app again

8.3 Exceptions

We may deny or delay deletion requests in limited circumstances:

  • Account is subject to ongoing legal proceedings
  • Outstanding financial obligations or disputes
  • Active security investigation
  • Required to comply with legal obligations

You will be notified if your deletion request cannot be processed immediately and provided with a timeframe.
