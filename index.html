Liftrix Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2024

Welcome to Liftrix. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our fitness tracking application ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Liftrix, you may provide us with the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, display name, and password (stored securely using industry-standard encryption)
  • Profile Information: Age, gender, fitness goals, profile picture, and bio
  • Workout Data: Exercise routines, sets, reps, weights, workout duration, rest times, and personal records
  • Health Metrics: Body measurements, weight tracking, calorie burn estimates, and performance analytics
  • Social Data: Posts, comments, likes, follows, gym buddy connections, and shared workout content
  • AI Chat Data: Conversations with our AI coaching assistant, including questions, preferences, and coaching recommendations
  • User-Generated Content: Photos, videos, text posts, and comments shared within the app

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, screen views, session duration, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Analytics Data: Interaction patterns, feature adoption, and aggregated usage statistics
Important: We do NOT collect location data. Any location permissions previously declared have been removed from our app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide workout tracking, analytics, progress monitoring, and personalized fitness insights
  • AI Coaching: To power our AI assistant with personalized workout recommendations and coaching advice using Firebase Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite
  • Social Features: To enable social interactions, workout sharing, gym buddy connections, and community engagement
  • Account Management: To authenticate users, maintain account security, and provide customer support
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix bugs, optimize app performance, and prevent crashes
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior, improve features, and develop new functionality
  • Communication: To send important notifications about your workouts, achievements, social interactions, and app updates
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

3. Third-Party Services

Liftrix integrates with the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

3.1 Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication with email/password and Google Sign-In
  • Cloud Firestore: Real-time database synchronization for workout data, social profiles, and user content
  • Firebase Storage: Secure storage for profile images and workout media
  • Firebase Analytics: App usage analytics and crash reporting (Crashlytics)
  • Firebase Performance: App performance monitoring and optimization
  • Firebase AI (Gemini): AI-powered coaching and workout recommendations
  • Firebase Remote Config: Feature flags and A/B testing

Firebase services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This is governed by Google's OAuth 2.0 policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption at Rest: All local database data is encrypted using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption in Transit: All network communications use TLS 1.3+ encryption
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data (tokens, credentials) stored using EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • Authentication: Secure Firebase Authentication with password hashing and token-based sessions
  • Code Protection: ProGuard/R8 code obfuscation to prevent reverse engineering
  • Backup Exclusion: Sensitive data excluded from Android backup systems

4.2 Data Storage Location

Your data is stored in:

  • Local Device: Encrypted SQLite database on your Android device (using Room with SQLCipher)
  • Cloud Storage: Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) servers in compliance with GDPR requirements

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as necessary to provide our services
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Workout History: Historical workout data is retained for analytics and personal progress tracking until account deletion
  • AI Chat History: Chat conversations are retained for service improvement and may be anonymized for AI model training
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Use the "Export Data" feature in Settings → Privacy & Security.

6.2 Right to Rectification

You can update your profile information, workout data, and account settings at any time within the app.

6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data through Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete Account.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your workout data in machine-readable format (JSON) using the "Export Data" feature.

6.5 Right to Restriction of Processing

You can limit how we use your data by:

  • Disabling analytics tracking in app settings
  • Opting out of AI coaching features
  • Making your profile private to restrict social data sharing

6.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain types of data processing, including:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring (disable in settings)
  • AI-powered coaching (disable AI features)
  • Social features and data sharing (set profile to private)

6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time by deleting your account or disabling specific features.

7. Health Data (Special Category Data)

GDPR Special Category Notice: Liftrix processes health-related data (workout performance, body measurements, calorie tracking) which is considered a "special category" of personal data under GDPR Article 9.

We process health data based on your explicit consent, which you provide when creating an account and using fitness tracking features. You can withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your account.

Health Data We Collect:

  • Weight and body measurements
  • Exercise performance metrics (1RM, volume, intensity)
  • Calorie burn estimates
  • Recovery metrics and rest times
  • Performance trends and analytics

How We Protect Health Data:

  • Stored with AES-256 encryption
  • Transmitted only over encrypted connections (TLS 1.3+)
  • Access restricted to essential app functionality
  • Never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes

8. Social Features and Data Sharing

8.1 Public vs. Private Data

  • Public Data: If you set your profile to "Public", your workout posts, profile information, and activity may be visible to all Liftrix users
  • Followers Only: Content shared with "Followers" privacy is visible only to users who follow you
  • Private: Private content is visible only to you

8.2 Gym Buddy Connections

When you connect with gym buddies via QR code pairing (maximum 5 buddies), you share workout data and personal records with those users. You can remove buddy connections at any time.

8.3 User-Generated Content

Content you post (workouts, comments, images) may be visible to other users based on your privacy settings. We implement content moderation to prevent inappropriate content, but you are responsible for what you share.

9. Children's Privacy

Liftrix is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@liftrix.app.

IARC Content Rating: Teen (13+) - Social features and user-generated content

10. Content Moderation

To maintain a safe community, we implement automated content moderation:

  • Profanity filtering for posts and comments
  • Spam detection
  • Reporting and blocking tools for users
  • Admin moderation for policy violations

Flagged content may be reviewed by our moderation team. Violations of our Community Guidelines may result in content removal or account suspension.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (Google Cloud Platform infrastructure). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Google Cloud Platform's GDPR compliance and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Adherence to EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework principles

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app

Your continued use of Liftrix after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Provide guidance on protective measures you can take

17. Do Not Track Signals

Our app does not currently respond to Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. You can control analytics tracking through in-app settings.

18. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@liftrix.app.

19. Consent Management

You can manage your privacy preferences and consent settings in the app:

  • Settings → Privacy & Security: Manage account privacy and data sharing
  • Settings → General → Analytics: Opt in/out of analytics tracking
  • Settings → AI Chat Settings: Control AI data usage and language preferences
  • Profile → Privacy: Set profile visibility (Public/Followers/Private)
